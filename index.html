How it works

Jump into canine health in 3 simple steps

01

Tell us what your pal likes (and what not)

Never again waste time thinking about what to buy! Poochi AI will create a 100% personalized weekly meal plan just for your dog. It makes sure they get all the nutrients and vitamins they need, no matter what type of diet they require!

02

Approve their weekly meal plan

Once per week, approve the meal plan generated for your pet by Poochi AI. You can change ingredients, swap entire meals, or even add your own recipes.

03

Receive meals at convenient time

Our talented chefs will cook your selected meal weekly, and we will deliver it to your door whenever works best for you. You can change delivery schedule and address daily!
